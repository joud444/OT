What is Occupational Therapy?

Occupational therapy is a healthcare profession that helps people of all ages participate in everyday activities. Occupational therapists assist those with physical, mental, or developmental disabilities to develop, recover, or maintain the skills needed for daily living.

OT involves a wide range of activities, such as:

  • Self-care (bathing, dressing, eating)
  • Productivity (work, school, volunteering)
  • Leisure (play, hobbies, social activities)

OT & Programming: Promising Horizons

Programming can develop innovative tools and solutions in OT, such as:

  • Interactive apps and games to enhance motor and cognitive skills.
  • Assistive devices and software for people with disabilities.
  • Systems to collect and analyze patient data to track progress and assess treatment effectiveness.

I'm interested in exploring these possibilities and developing my programming skills to serve the OT field.
